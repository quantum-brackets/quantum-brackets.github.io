Rates

Depending on the service, we offer an hourly rate or a flat rate. Our consultants keep detailed logs of the time they spend developing your solutions. We count in ten minute increments and round down when in-between them. Work logs will be disclosed to all clients upon final billing. We only work for as long as a job requires, so you’ll never be overbilled for unproductive time. Final bills will include costs for copy, jobs done in service, and third-party research tools. Our rates fall between $100 and $200 per hour.
